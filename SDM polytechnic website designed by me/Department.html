Computer Science & Engineering

The Computer Science & Engineering department focuses on the study of computers and computational systems. It covers areas such as algorithms, programming, data structures, artificial intelligence, and software engineering. The department aims to provide students with a solid foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of computing.

Electronics & Communication Engineering

The Electronics & Communication Engineering department deals with the electronic devices, circuits, communication equipment like transmitter, receiver, integrated circuits (IC). It also aims to deepen the knowledge and skills of the students on the basic concepts and theories that will equip them in their professional work involving analysis, systems implementation, operation, production, and maintenance of the various applications in the field of Electronics and Communications Engineering.

Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering department is concerned with the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment, including works like roads, bridges, canals, dams, and buildings. This department focuses on equipping students with the necessary skills to solve complex problems related to construction and infrastructure development.

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering department applies engineering physics, engineering mathematics, and materials science principles to design, analyze, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems.
